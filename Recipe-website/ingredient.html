Recipe 1: Chicken Biryani

Preparation Time:

15 minutes

Cooking Time:

5 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb flank or skirt steak
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together orange juice, lime juice, garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place steak in a shallow dish and pour marinade over it. Turn to coat both sides. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 4 hours.
  3. Preheat grill to high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  4. Remove steak from marinade and discard excess marinade. Grill steak for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or to your desired level of doneness.
  5. Transfer steak to a cutting board and let it rest for 5 minutes. Slice thinly against the grain and serve.

Recipe 2: Dosa

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup Parboiled Rice (idli-dosa rice)
  • 3/4 cup Regular Rice

  • 1/4 teaspoon Fenugreek Seeds (methi dana)
  • 1/2 tablespoon Chana Dal (gram lentils), optional
  • Water, as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil, for shallow frying
